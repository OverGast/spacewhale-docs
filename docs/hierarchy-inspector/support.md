# Support

Two ways to reach us when something isn't working, when you want a feature, or when you just have a question.

## Open a GitHub issue

For **bugs**, **feature requests**, or **questions about how something works**:

**[github.com/OverGast/Hierarchy-Inspector/issues](https://github.com/OverGast/Hierarchy-Inspector/issues)**

This is the best channel for anything reproducible. Public threads also help future users find answers without having to ask again.

A good bug report includes:

- Unity version (e.g. Unity 6000.0.32f1)
- Hierarchy Inspector version (visible at the top of the theme inspector, or in the welcome window)
- A short description of what you expected vs. what you saw
- Reproduction steps
- A screenshot if it's a visual issue

## Email

For **license questions**, **purchase or account problems**, or anything you'd rather not discuss in public:

**[spacewhale.assets@gmail.com](mailto:spacewhale.assets@gmail.com)**

We aim to reply within a few business days.

## Before you reach out

A quick check that often saves a round-trip:

1. Make sure **Overlay Enabled** is on at the top of the theme inspector. If the master switch is off, no Hierarchy Inspector features render.
2. Confirm the **active theme** is set in **Edit → Preferences → Hierarchy Inspector**. If the theme field is empty, nothing renders.
3. Try the bundled **HierarchyTheme-Default**. If a custom theme misbehaves, swapping back to default usually narrows the issue down to a setting.
4. Skim the relevant **Features** or **Theme Reference** page in the sidebar; the answer to "how do I X" is often a sentence in the docs.
